# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks or intimidation
- doxxing or privacy violations
- bad-faith disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit/remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation when necessary to protect contributors.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to: `conduct@maild.click` (replace if needed).
